About

Howdy! My name is George McLarry! I was raised on a Huon Valley farm in Tasmania, Australia. Self-taught on guitar since age 10, my music draws from the simplicity and Christian roots of old-style country, inspired by legends like Hank Sr., Paul Overstreet, and Slim Dusty. In 2025, I went all the way up to Tamworth to busk, but finding a stage, I ended up winning a guitar for being voted as the best walk-up artist! My songs carry the honest, small town-spirit for all y'all who still love and remember old style country music - real country!
