About Southern Cross Rangers

We are a country music band from Tasmania, Australia, dedicated to preserving and sharing the authentic sounds of our homeland. Our music is inspired by the rugged beauty of Tasmania's landscapes, the resilience of its people, and the rich heritage of Australian country music.
